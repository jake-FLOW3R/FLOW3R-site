Privacy Policy for FLOW3R Solutions

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

FLOW3R Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") operates a business management platform for service businesses. This policy explains how we collect and use data.

What we collect

Business contact information, customer lead data entered by our clients, and OAuth tokens for third-party integrations (e.g. QuickBooks).

How we use it

To provide the FLOW3R platform to our clients. We do not sell data to third parties.

QuickBooks integration

If you connect QuickBooks, we store an OAuth access token to sync data on your behalf. You can disconnect at any time from the Settings page.

Data security

Data is stored on Railway's managed PostgreSQL infrastructure. Access is restricted by business account.
